model learns by interacting with an environment,  
and receiving penalties or rewards based on its 
actions. Self-driving cars and game AIs are great  
applications of this. Finally, deep learning uses 
multi-layered neural networks, inspired by how the  
human brain works, to handle large datasets and 
perform complex tasks; more than is typically  
possible with other forms of machine learning.

examples. And generative AI uses machine learning  
to learn patterns from existing data and create 
new content, such as text and images. ChatGPT  
and other popular large language models are good 
examples of this. In networking, a few possible  
applications are configuration generation, 
troubleshooting, and script generation.
